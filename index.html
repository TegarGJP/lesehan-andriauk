Our Story

Since 2005

Founded by Chef André Akhunov, Andri Auk has been serving the freshest seafood to discerning diners for nearly two decades. What began as a small bistro has grown into an award-winning establishment recognized for its innovative approach to seafood cuisine.

We source our ingredients from sustainable fisheries and local fishermen who share our commitment to quality and environmental responsibility. Each dish tells a story of the sea, prepared with respect for tradition and a touch of modern flair.
